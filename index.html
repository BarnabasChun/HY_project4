About

If you've exhausted every list of "Best Worst Movies", find Tommy Wiseau's The Room to be a masterpiece, or are an adamant supporter of Nicolas Cage's illustrious career, then this app will fill the void in your your life and help you indulge in your guilty pleasure of crappy movies. This app works by pulling data from TMdB's API, with movies rated poorly by their users being randomly selected, depending on your preference of genre and rating.

This product uses the TMDb API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDb.
